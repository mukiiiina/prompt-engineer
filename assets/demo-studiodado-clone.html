Studio DADO

Founded in 2014, Studio DADO is a Miami-based interior design studio specializing in hospitality and residential spaces. We are a team of 12 designers, architects, and storytellers united by a singular belief: that every space should feel deeply human.

Our work spans luxury resorts, boutique hotels, private residences, and restaurants across the Americas, Europe, and the Caribbean.
